A Little About Us

Beats and Deets is a Chicago based company that offers a wide range of wedding and event detailing and planning solutions. Adam Ryan, founder, received a Bachelor’s of Fine Art with a focus on Graphic Design and Photography from Illinois State University. He has worked in the creative industry as an Art Director for 14 years and as a wedding and event DJ for 20 years, which gives him the ability to bring an innovative perspective to anything you might need.

While providing DJ services for over 250 weddings and events, we have seen many people stress over making sure their day is perfect, often spending months meeting with various vendors. We wanted to simplify the process so we have teamed up with the best in the industry and formed one company that could meet all your needs making the planning process much more efficient and STRESS FREE. Rather you hire us for just one detail or have us handle the whole event, we are sure to make your event an absolute success. We offer a diverse mix of services including DJ’s, Uplighting, Musicians, Photography, Wedding Officiating, Photobooth Rental and Staffing, Event Planning/Coordination, Invitation/Program Design, Theme Development, Video Production, Custom Event Website Design and more! We bring all the beats and deets together as one team to maximize the impact of your event. It’s the small details that will bring out your big day, and that is what we thrive on.
